Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to KinetraX. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

KinetraX is operated by KinetraX Inc. and provides a blockchain-based fitness platform connecting trainers and athletes.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile photos.
  • Fitness Data: Training history, performance metrics, and scheduled events.
  • Payment Information: Wallet addresses (but not private keys).
  • Verification Data: For trainers, we collect credentials and certifications.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and device type.
  • Location Data: For mapping and event discovery features.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving our services
  • Matching athletes with trainers
  • Processing payments via TON blockchain
  • Verifying trainer credentials
  • Personalizing your experience
  • Analytics and platform improvement

4. Blockchain-Specific Disclosures

Please be aware that blockchain transactions are public and immutable. Information stored on the blockchain cannot be changed or deleted. We store the minimum necessary information on-chain, with most personal data stored off-chain in secure databases.

KinetraX token transactions are visible on the TON blockchain explorer.

5. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Trainers/athletes (as needed for service functionality)
  • Payment processors or blockchain networks
  • Service providers (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (with blockchain limitations explained)
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@kinetrax.io.

7. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We recommend using strong passwords for your wallet and following best practices for blockchain security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
