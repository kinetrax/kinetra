Whitepaper

Executive Summary

KinetraX is a blockchain-based fitness platform that connects trainers and athletes using TON blockchain technology. Our platform enables secure verification of trainers, transparent payment processing, and incentivizes participation through the KinetraX token.

By leveraging blockchain technology, we eliminate intermediaries, reduce fees, and create a trustless environment for fitness services.

Market Analysis

The global fitness industry is valued at over $100 billion, with a growing demand for personalized training services. However, the industry faces challenges:

  • Lack of transparency in trainer qualifications
  • High fees charged by intermediary platforms
  • Payment processing delays and disputes

KinetraX addresses these challenges through blockchain verification, smart contracts, and direct peer-to-peer connections.

Platform Overview

KinetraX provides a comprehensive ecosystem for fitness services:

For Trainers

  • Blockchain verification of credentials
  • Create and manage events with flexible pricing
  • Automatic payments through smart contracts
  • Build reputation through transparent reviews

For Athletes

  • Find verified trainers by location
  • Secure payments with escrow protection
  • Discounts when paying with KinetraX tokens
  • Access to workout history and performance metrics

Blockchain Implementation

KinetraX is built on the TON blockchain, chosen for its high throughput, low transaction costs, and robust smart contract capabilities.

Smart Contracts

Our platform utilizes several key smart contracts:

  • Trainer Verification Contract: Stores and verifies trainer credentials
  • Event Management Contract: Handles event creation, booking, and cancellations
  • Payment Processing Contract: Manages escrow, releases funds upon completion

Token Economics

The KinetraX token (KTX) plays a central role in our ecosystem:

  • Discounts on platform services when paying with KTX
  • Staking rewards for token holders
  • Governance voting on platform development

Token Distribution

Total Supply: 21 million KTX tokens

  • 40% - User rewards and ecosystem growth
  • 20% - Team and advisors (vested over 3 years)
  • 15% - Development fund
  • 15% - Marketing and partnerships
  • 10% - Reserve
